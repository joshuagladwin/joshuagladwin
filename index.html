Well hello there!

Welcome to my website. My name's Josh. I'm a Data Nerd and I live in Berlin with my husband Chris and my cat Sulu.

I have a diverse academic background, with a Bachelors Degree in Japanese and Chinese (including a year out living in Hokkaido) and a Postgraduate Diploma in Linguistics. I love languages - and I mean LOVE languages. My many bookshelves at home have books from Irish to Korean, Japanese to German and Estonian to Thai!

Whether it's languages, numbers, patterns, or whatever - data has always fascinated me. So I decided to make the leap a while back and train in data. I taught myself everything, and now I could really do with helping somebody else with their data needs. If I sound like the sort of person you're looking for, please get in touch!

I like to think I've achieved a lot of things outside of my data interests. My husband and I together developed the LobbyALord platform which helped get the UK's same-sex marriage bill through the House of Lords. It was so effective, we were even thanked by the Prime Minister for it.
